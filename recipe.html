Jollof spaghetti

How to cook jollof spagetti. A dish different for the normal plain spagetti with stew or sauce. In this case we'll be mixing the sauce with the plain white spaghetti to get JOLLOF SPAGHETTI.

Recipe Information

  • Time of preperation 15 minutes
  • Cooking time 35 minutes
  • Servings: 2 plates
  • Difficulty Level: Beginner

Ingridients

  • Spaghetti 1 pack
  • Tin tomatoes 2 cans medium sized
  • 3 Cubes of Maggi Seasoning
  • 4 Tea Spoons of Salt
  • 41/2 Tablespoon of Powedered pepper
  • A few proteins(Eggs, beef, chicken.)

    • COOKING GUIDELINES

    1. Boil about 4-6 litres of water for about 10 minutes
    2. When the water is already boiling add the pack of sagetti and leave to cook for 12 minutes
    3. While the water is boilin take a pot and add the 2 cans of tin tomatoes in it add your seasonings(maggi, pepper, salt etc) also add some groundnut oil, mix everthing and fry.
    4. Take a sieve and drained any excess water from the spagetti then add to the mixture you fried and mix everthing together.
    5. Add your proteins into your spagetti.
    6. Serve hot on a plate with a chilled drink if preferred.
